What we promise

How it's made

The same order every batch, so what's in the bag never changes.

Harvest

Cut fresh from the field the same morning

Wash

Rinsed clean, no soaking, no chemicals

Sundry

Slow dried in shade to hold colour & nutrients

Stone grind

Milled cool, so nothing is cooked out

Sieve

Fine sifted for a smooth, lump free powder

Pack

Sealed same day, straight to your door

Certified & Compliant

Made under registered food safety standards.

Every batch is prepared, packed, and shipped under our FSSAI license no shortcuts on hygiene, no gaps in traceability for our organic nutrition products.
